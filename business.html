Business Analytics

Business Analytics equips students with the skills to analyze and interpret data to support business decision-making across various industries. It emphasizes key analytic methods, data mining techniques, and the creation of effective visualizations for data-driven insights.

Concepts

  • Explore the role of business analytics in today’s data-driven environment.
  • Identify key business analytics terminology and key performance indicators across industries.
  • Examine business practices that lead to effective use of analytics within organizations.

Analytical Methods and Data Types

  • Explore descriptive, predictive, and prescriptive analytics methods.
  • Apply basic measures and distribution methods to summarize and modify data.
  • Use spreadsheet functions, including logical functions, data validation, and financial functions.

Data Mining and Decision Support

Project Management

Project Management covers key competencies in managing projects, including lifecycle phases, team leadership, stakeholder management, and risk assessment. It prepares students to utilize tools and methodologies to ensure project success, from initiation to closure.

Concepts

Project Management Profession and Framework

  • Examine the role of the Project Manager and the Project Management Office (PMO).
  • Discuss the importance of the Project Management Institute (PMI) and project structures.
  • Understand the phases of the project life cycle and how they align with business strategy.

Team Leadership and Stakeholder Management

  • Explore team leadership principles and attributes of high-performing teams.
  • Identify internal and external stakeholders and discuss stakeholder communication tools.
  • Assess team performance, practice negotiation, and conflict management techniques.

Project Planning and Proposal Development

  • Develop project proposals and explore the project selection process.
  • Write a project charter and understand its components.
  • Produce a Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) and use project management tools for planning.

Risk Management and Project Closure

  • Create risk management strategies, identify key risks, and develop contingency plans.
  • Discuss the importance of quality standards, cost, and schedule controls.
  • Facilitate project post-mortem processes and prepare closing documents and reports.
